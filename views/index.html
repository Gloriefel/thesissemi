Commission on Higher Education (CHED) Apply Now!

CHED Scholarship Programs (CSPs) is previously referred as Student Financial Assistance Programs (StuFAPs). The applications are open to high school graduates, candidate for graduation, college unit earners, passers of Alternative Learning System (ALS) and Philippine Educational Placement Test (PEPT) passers.

No exam needed!
Criteria of Eligibility:
  • Filipino citizen;
  • Graduating senior highschool student/highschool graduate with general weighted average (GWA) of atleast 85%;
  • Parent/s or guardian Annual Gross income of Four Hundred Thousand Pesos (Php 400,000.00) and below.
    • Academic Requirements - any one of the following:
  • High school report card
  • Duly certified true copy of grades for Grade 11 or 1st semester of Grade 12 for graduating highschool students.
    • Income Requirements - any one of the following:
  • Lastest ITR of parents or guardian if employed
  • Certified of Tax Exemption from the BIR
  • Certified of Indigency from their Barangay
  • Certificate/Case Study from DSWD
  • Latest copy of contract or proof of income for children of Overseas Filipino Workers (OFW) and seafarers.

    Apply Now! : TWSP Scholarship STEP Scholarship

    Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) is offering Skills Training and Scholarship programs in 2020. It is open to everyone including OFWs. Interested applicants should apply online to avail of the scholarship programs under the Technical Vocational Education and Training (TVET).

    No exam needed!
    Who can apply?

    TESDA extends financial assistance to marginalized but deserving students in post-secondary non-degree courses; promote TVET; contribute to the development of a competent skilled workforce;
